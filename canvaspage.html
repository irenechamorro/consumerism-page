CANVAS REFORMER

This project was made in 2021 as a response to the stress of finals week on college students.

An experiemental google chrome extension was created that removes all text, assignments, and

grades from one's Canvas page, thus removing all stressors from a college student's life.

Although not very practical, this project gives a new meaning to "what you don't know cannot

hurt you," especially in a digital age where all information is instantatous and news can be

known immediately, good or bad.

❮
❯

To make the extension I used very simple CSS to remove all the text on the page.

The graphics were made to highlight and accompany the simplicity of the extension,

including the green canvas logo, which communicates the positivity behind it, as

opposed to the brooding red of the orginal Canvas logo.
